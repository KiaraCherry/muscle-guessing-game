About Musclele

Musclele is a Wordle-style anatomy game designed to help physiotherapy students learn and retain muscle knowledge in a fun, daily format.

Each round presents anatomical clues — function, origin, insertion, innervation, and clinical notes — progressively revealed with each wrong guess.

Built with Node.js, MongoDB, and vanilla JavaScript. Designed with physio students in mind.

Made by Kiara Cherry · v0.1.0 beta

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

What we collect

When you create an account we store your display name, email address, and a securely hashed password. We also store your game statistics — streaks, win rates, guess distributions, and accuracy by muscle group.

How we use it

Your data is used solely to power the app — saving your progress, displaying your stats, and showing the leaderboard. We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising.

Guest users

If you play as a guest, no personal data is collected or stored. Game results are not saved.

Data storage

Data is stored securely on MongoDB Atlas. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and are never stored in plain text.

Contact

For any privacy questions, contact us at your@email.com.
